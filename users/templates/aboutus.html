Transforming Communities Through Sustainable Action

Live Great Foundation (LGF) is a community-driven initiative committed to transforming urban slums into cleaner, more sustainable, and empowered spaces.

Who We Are

Through innovative waste management programs, climate education, and economic empowerment initiatives, we are creating a ripple effect of positive change.

Where We Started

Our journey began in Kibera, Kenya's largest informal settlement, and we are expanding our impact across Nairobi and beyond, reaching communities in Kiambu, Siaya, Turkana, and Homabay counties.
