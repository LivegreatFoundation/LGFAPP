The Problem

A Waste Crisis in Urban Slums

Urban slums like Kibera face a growing waste management crisis, where uncollected garbage clogs streets, pollutes waterways, and poses serious health risks. Limited infrastructure, lack of awareness, and economic hardships make proper waste disposal nearly impossible.

The consequences?

Environmental Degradation – Unchecked waste leads to pollution and poor sanitation.

Health Hazards – Accumulated trash attracts pests, spreads diseases, and worsens air quality.

Missed Economic Opportunities – Valuable recyclable materials are discarded instead of repurposed.

Lack of Climate Education – Many communities are unaware of how waste impacts their environment.

Our Solution

Waste to Opportunity

At Live Great Foundation, we don't just collect waste—we create solutions that empower communities.

Sustainable Waste Management

We offer waste collection, segregation, and plastic resale to reduce pollution.

Employment for Youth

By turning waste into work, we create jobs and provide a steady income for young people.

Climate Education

We train children and communities to become Climate Champions, driving long-term change.

Supporting Local Sports

We combine environmental efforts with nutritional support for young athletes, ensuring a healthier future.

By embracing a circular economy, we are transforming waste into a resource, restoring the environment, and building a model for sustainable urban living.
